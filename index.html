Jeffrey Ross

Operations Leader Systems Builder Army Veteran

I am a systems-oriented operations leader and U.S. Army Veteran with over 15 years of experience driving operational excellence, designing workflow automation, and orchestrating teams in high-stress, high-scale environments.

My background is built on two core phases: serving in the U.S. Army as a Non-Rated Crew Member Flight Instructor and Crew Chief with specialized experience in Blackhawk helicopter systems, and executing rapid growth strategies in the private sector. As General Manager of Jason Ross Auto Sales, I engineered the operational systems that scaled dealership inventory from 8 to over 120 active vehicles and managed approximately $1 million in average monthly gross revenue.

I specialize in bridging the gap between operations and emerging technology—deploying artificial intelligence applications, designing self-hosted architectures, and integrating robust digital systems to eliminate inefficiencies and scale organizational capacity.
